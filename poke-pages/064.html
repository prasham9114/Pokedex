Kadabra

Kadabra is the evolved form of Abra, a Psychic-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Psi Pokémon. Kadabra emits strong alpha waves from its body that induce headaches to those nearby. It possesses strong psychic abilities.
