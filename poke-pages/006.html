Charizard

Charizard is the final evolution of Charmander, a Fire/Flying-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Flame Pokémon. Charizard's fiery breath can melt almost anything.

Height: 1.7m | Weight: 90.5kg

Inherent Ability: Blaze
