Kabutops

Kabutops is a Rock/Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Shellfish Pokémon. Kabutops was originally a Pokémon that lived in the sea but it later adapted to life on land. It has scythe-like arms that it uses to catch prey.
