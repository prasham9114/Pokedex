Poliwrath

Poliwrath is the evolved form of Poliwhirl, a Water/Fighting-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Tadpole Pokémon. Poliwrath is known for its incredible strength and fighting prowess. It's said to be capable of smashing concrete blocks with its powerful arms.
