Horsea

Horsea is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Dragon Pokémon. Horsea swims by cleverly flapping the fins on its back. It nests in the shade of corals where it is protected from the sea's currents.

Height: 0.4m | Weight: 8.0kg

Inherent Ability: Swift Swim | Sniper (Hidden Ability)
