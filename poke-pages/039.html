Jigglypuff

Jigglypuff is a Normal/Fairy-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Balloon Pokémon. It captivates opponents with its huge, round eyes and sings a soothing lullaby. While it sings, it doesn't notice foes approaching and can easily become prey.
