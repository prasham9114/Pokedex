Ninetales

Ninetales is the evolved form of Vulpix, a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Fox Pokémon. It is said to live for a thousand years and possesses nine tails that are said to be imbued with a mystic power. It can manipulate them to create a wondrous display of mystical flames.

Height: 1.1m | Weight: 19.9kg

Inherent Ability: Flash Fire | Drought (Hidden Ability)
