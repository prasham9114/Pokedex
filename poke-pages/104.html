Cubone

Cubone is a Ground-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Lonely Pokémon. Cubone wears the skull of its deceased mother on its head at all times. It cries mournfully in the moonlight.

Height: 0.4m | Weight: 6.5kg

Inherent Ability: Rock Head | Lightning Rod (Hidden Ability)

Weakness:
