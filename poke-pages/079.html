Slowpoke

Slowpoke is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Dopey Pokémon. Slowpoke is often found lazing at the water's edge. It evolves into Slowbro starting at level 37 or Slowking when traded holding a King's Rock.
