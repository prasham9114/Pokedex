Vaporeon

Vaporeon is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Bubble Jet Pokémon. Vaporeon's body is light blue with a dark blue marking around its head and another marking around its neck that resembles a collar. It has a fin on its head resembling its ears and a tuft of white fur on its tail.

Height: 1.0m | Weight: 29.0kg

Inherent Ability: Water Absorb | Hydration (Hidden Ability)

Type:

  • Water

Weakness:

  • Electric
  • Grass
