Arbok

Arbok is the evolved form of Ekans, a Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Cobra Pokémon. The pattern on its belly appears to be a frightening face. Weak foes will flee just at the sight of the pattern.

Height: 3.5m | Weight: 65.0kg

Inherent Ability: Intimidate | Shed Skin (Hidden Ability)
