Alakazam

Alakazam is the evolved form of Kadabra, a Psychic-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Psi Pokémon. Alakazam has an IQ of 5000 and possesses immense psychic powers. It can use its psychic abilities to create and control a spoon made of pure psychic energy.
