Mankey

Mankey is a Fighting-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Pig Monkey Pokémon. Mankey's rage peaks when it sees a slight movement. It's known for its aggressive nature and is quick to start fights. Its kicks are powerful and can send foes flying.
