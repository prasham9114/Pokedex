Kingler

Kingler is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Pincer Pokémon. Kingler has an enormous, oversized claw. It can wave this massive claw in the air to communicate with others. However, because the claw is so heavy, the Pokémon quickly tires.

Height: 1.3m | Weight: 60.0kg

Inherent Ability: Hyper Cutter | Shell Armor (Hidden Ability)

Type:

  • Water

Weakness:

  • Electric
  • Grass
