Gastly

Gastly is a Ghost/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Gas Pokémon. Gastly is mostly composed of gaseous matter. It is invisible in the dark, and it can absorb anything by enveloping the target entirely.

Height: 1.3m | Weight: 0.1kg

Inherent Ability: Levitate
