Seaking

Seaking is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Goldfish Pokémon. Seaking is very proud of its horn, which is said to have impressive powers. In the wild, Seaking compete for territory using their horn's size as a display of strength.

Height: 1.3m | Weight: 39.0kg

Inherent Ability: Swift Swim | Water Veil (Hidden Ability)
