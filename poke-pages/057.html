Primeape

Primeape is the evolved form of Mankey, a Fighting-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Pig Monkey Pokémon. Primeape has a terrible temper and is known for its frequent bouts of rage. It becomes uncontrollable if angered and can cause significant damage.
