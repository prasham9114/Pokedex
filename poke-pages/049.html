Venomoth

Venomoth is the evolved form of Venonat, a Bug/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Poison Moth Pokémon. Venomoth is nocturnal - it is attracted by bright lights at night. It flutters around in the dark, covered in toxic powder.

Height: 1.5m | Weight: 12.5kg

Inherent Ability: Shield Dust | Tinted Lens (Hidden Ability)
