Clefairy

Clefairy is a Fairy-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Fairy Pokémon. Clefairy moves by skipping lightly as if it were flying using its wings. Its bouncy step lets it even walk on water. It is known to have a sweet temperament.
