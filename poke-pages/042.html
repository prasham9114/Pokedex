Golbat

Golbat is the evolved form of Zubat, a Poison/Flying-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Bat Pokémon. Golbat loves to drink the blood of living things. It is particularly active in the pitch black of night, when it seeks its prey with its ultrasonic cries.

Height: 1.6m | Weight: 55.0kg

Inherent Ability: Inner Focus | Infiltrator (Hidden Ability)
