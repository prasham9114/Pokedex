Nidoqueen

Nidoqueen is the evolved form of Nidorina, a dual-type Poison/Ground Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Drill Pokémon. Nidoqueen's body is encased in extremely hard scales. It is adept at sending foes flying with harsh tackles.
