Golduck

Golduck is the evolved form of Psyduck, a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Duck Pokémon. Golduck is a strong swimmer and is capable of swimming nonstop up fast-moving rivers at a steady speed of eight knots.
