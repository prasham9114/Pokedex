Hypno

Hypno is a Psychic-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Hypnosis Pokémon. Hypno holds a pendulum in its hand. The arcing movement and glitter of the pendulum lull the foe into a deep state of hypnosis. While they sleep, Hypno feeds on their dreams.

Height: 1.6m | Weight: 75.6kg

Inherent Ability: Insomnia | Forewarn | Inner Focus (Hidden Ability)

Type:

  • Psychic

Weakness:

  • Bug
  • Ghost
  • Dark
