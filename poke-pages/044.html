Oddish

Oddish is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Weed Pokémon. During the day, Oddish buries itself in soil to absorb nutrients from the ground using its entire body. It blooms when the sun goes down, releasing a strong aroma.

Height: 0.5m | Weight: 5.4kg

Inherent Ability: Chlorophyll | Run Away (Hidden Ability)

Type:

  • Poison

Weakness:

  • Flying

  • Psychic
