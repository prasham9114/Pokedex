Diglett

Diglett is a Ground-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Mole Pokémon. Diglett lives underground, sensing its surroundings through vibrations in the ground. If it senses danger, it will quickly burrow to safety at a depth of 60 miles underground.
