Eevee

Eevee is a Normal-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Evolution Pokémon. Eevee has an unstable genetic makeup that allows it to evolve into one of several different Pokémon depending on its environment. It is said to have the potential to evolve into any Pokémon.
