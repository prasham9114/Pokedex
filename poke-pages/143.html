Snorlax

Snorlax is a Normal-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Sleeping Pokémon. Snorlax is very lazy and sleeps for long periods in order to digest its food. It is a heavy eater and can consume food equivalent to its own weight in a single day.
