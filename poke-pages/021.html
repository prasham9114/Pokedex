Spearow

Spearow is a dual-type Normal/Flying Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Tiny Bird Pokémon. Spearow has a very loud cry that can be heard over half a mile away. If its high, keening cry is heard echoing all around, it is a sign that they are warning each other of danger.

Height: 0.3m | Weight: 2.0kg

Inherent Ability: Keen Eye
