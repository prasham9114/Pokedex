Parasect

Parasect is the evolved form of Paras, a Bug/Grass-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Mushroom Pokémon. Once the mushroom on its back grows larger than Paras, it drains nutrients from the bug's body. Parasect controls the bug by emitting spores.
