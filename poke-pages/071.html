Victreebel

Victreebel is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Flycatcher Pokémon. Victreebel has a large mouth with razor-sharp leaves, allowing it to ensnare prey. It camouflages itself as a plant to avoid detection.
