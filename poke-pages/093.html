Haunter

Haunter is a Ghost/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Gas Pokémon. Haunter likes to lurk in the dark and play mischievous tricks on people. It can pass through walls and can emit a faint cold, chilly breeze when nearby.
