Mr. Mime

Mr. Mime is a Psychic/Fairy-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Barrier Pokémon. Mr. Mime is capable of creating invisible walls made of its psychic powers. This Pokémon will mime its invisible object to protect its territory.
