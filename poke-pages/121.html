Starmie

Starmie is a Water/Psychic-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Mysterious Pokémon. Starmie's core shines bright in seven colors if it is sending out strong energy. Because of its luminous nature, this Pokémon has been given the nickname "the gem of the sea."
