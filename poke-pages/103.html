Exeggutor

Exeggutor is a Grass/Psychic-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Coconut Pokémon. Exeggutor is a Pokémon that resembles a palm tree. It is said that when the heads fall off, they group together to form Exeggcute.
