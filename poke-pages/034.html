Nidoking

Nidoking is the evolved form of Nidorino, a dual-type Poison/Ground Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Drill Pokémon. Nidoking's thick tail packs enormously destructive power capable of toppling a metal transmission tower. Once it goes on a rampage, there is no stopping it.
