Poliwhirl

Poliwhirl is the evolved form of Poliwag, a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Tadpole Pokémon. Poliwhirl's skin is so slippery, it's virtually impossible to grasp hold of it. It's said that Poliwhirl is adept at swimming and can even swim up waterfalls.

Height: 1.0m | Weight: 20.0kg

Inherent Ability: Water Absorb | Damp (Hidden Ability)

Type:

Weakness:

  • Grass
