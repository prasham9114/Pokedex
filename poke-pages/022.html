Fearow

Fearow is the evolved form of Spearow, a dual-type Normal/Flying Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Beak Pokémon. Fearow is recognized by its long neck and elongated beak. They are conveniently shaped for catching prey in soil or water.
