Raichu

Raichu is the evolved form of Pikachu, an Electric-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Mouse Pokémon. If it stores too much electricity, its behavior turns aggressive. To avoid this, it occasionally discharges excess energy and calms itself down.
