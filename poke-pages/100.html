Voltorb

Voltorb is an Electric-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Ball Pokémon. Voltorb is extremely sensitive—it explodes at the slightest of shocks. It is rumored that it was first created when a Poké Ball was exposed to a powerful pulse of energy.
