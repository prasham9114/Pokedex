Meowth

Meowth is a Normal-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Scratch Cat Pokémon. Meowth loves to roam at night to gather coins and other objects that sparkle. It's said to be attracted to shiny things and is known for its ability to pick up and hoard coins.
