Tentacruel

Tentacruel is a Water/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Jellyfish Pokémon. Tentacruel's body is composed primarily of water, allowing it to float effortlessly in the ocean. It uses its tentacles to capture prey and defend itself.
