Paras

Paras is a Bug/Grass-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Mushroom Pokémon. Paras is known to live in the damp soil of forests. It is often seen with a mushroom growing on its back, which feeds on the bug host. The mushroom releases toxic spores.
