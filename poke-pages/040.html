Wigglytuff

Wigglytuff is the evolved form of Jigglypuff, a Normal/Fairy-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Balloon Pokémon. Wigglytuff's fur is extremely flexible and elastic. By inhaling deeply, it can inflate itself seemingly without end, allowing it to bounce around like a balloon.
