Dugtrio

Dugtrio is the evolved form of Diglett, a Ground-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Mole Pokémon. Dugtrio's three heads move non-stop, combing the ground in search of food. It's said that if you find one, two more will spring up nearby.
