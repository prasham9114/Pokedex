Growlithe

Growlithe is a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Puppy Pokémon. Growlithe is loyal and friendly to its trainer, and it will bark fiercely at any opponent to protect its territory. Its fur is especially prized for its beauty and warmth.

Height: 0.7m | Weight: 19.0kg

Inherent Ability: Intimidate | Flash Fire (Hidden Ability)
