Onix

Onix is a Rock/Ground-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Rock Snake Pokémon. Onix has a magnet in its brain that serves as an internal compass, which allows it to know where it is heading underground. It tunnels through the ground at over 50 miles per hour.
