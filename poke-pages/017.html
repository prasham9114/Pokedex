Pidgeotto

Pidgeotto is the evolved form of Pidgey, a dual-type Normal/Flying Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Bird Pokémon. Pidgeotto claims a large area as its own territory. This Pokémon flies around, patrolling its living space. If its territory is violated, it shows no mercy in thoroughly punishing the foe with its sharp claws.

Height: 1.1m | Weight: 30.0kg

Inherent Ability: Keen Eye | Tangled Feet (Hidden Ability)

Type:

  • Flying

Weakness:

  • Ice
