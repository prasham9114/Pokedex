Venonat

Venonat is a Bug/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Insect Pokémon. Venonat's large eyes act as radar units. In a bright place, you can see that they are clusters of many tiny eyes. Its eyesight is so acute that even in total darkness, it can sense its prey.

Height: 1.0m | Weight: 30.0kg

Inherent Ability: Compound Eyes | Tinted Lens (Hidden Ability)

Type:

  • Poison

Weakness:

  • Fire
  • Flying
  • Psychic
