Kabuto

Kabuto is a Rock/Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Shellfish Pokémon. Kabuto is a Pokémon that has been regenerated from a fossil. It lived in the sea during ancient times and walked on land using its four legs.
