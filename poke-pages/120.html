Staryu

Staryu is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Star Shape Pokémon. Staryu's center section has an organ called the core that shines bright red. If you go to a beach toward the end of summer, the glowing cores of these Pokémon look like the stars in the sky.
