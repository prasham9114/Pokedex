Poliwag

Poliwag is a Water-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Tadpole Pokémon. Poliwag has a very thin skin, and the surface of its body is always wet with moisture. It can be found in ponds and lakes.

Height: 0.6m | Weight: 12.4kg

Inherent Ability: Water Absorb | Damp (Hidden Ability)

Type:

  • Water

Weakness:

  • Electric
  • Grass
