Pinsir

Pinsir is a Bug-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Stag Beetle Pokémon. Pinsir has a pair of massive horns. Protruding from the surface of these horns are thorns. These thorns are driven deeply into the opponent's body when the Pokémon attacks. The infested opponent is unable to flee from battle.

Height: 1.5m | Weight: 55.0kg

Inherent Ability: Hyper Cutter | Mold Breaker | Moxie (Hidden Ability)
