Gengar

Gengar is a Ghost/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Shadow Pokémon. Gengar is extremely mischievous and enjoys playing wicked pranks on people. It can absorb any surrounding heat, leaving a sudden chill, and it can enter and hide in the shadows of people, causing them to shake uncontrollably.
