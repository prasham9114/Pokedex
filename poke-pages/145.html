Zapdos

Zapdos is an Electric/Flying-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Electric Pokémon. Zapdos is a legendary bird Pokémon that has the ability to control electricity. It is said to appear from clouds while dropping enormous lightning bolts.
