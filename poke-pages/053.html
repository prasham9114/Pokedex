Persian

Persian is the evolved form of Meowth, a Normal-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Classy Cat Pokémon. Persian has a violent temperament, and it's fiercely territorial. Despite its appearance, it's a very agile and fast Pokémon.
