Muk

Muk is a Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Sludge Pokémon. Muk's body contains toxic substances, which it leaks from its skin. The stench of Muk's body can cause headaches and nausea.

Inherent Ability: Stench | Poison Touch (Hidden Ability)
