Arcanine

Arcanine is the evolved form of Growlithe, a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Legendary Pokémon. Arcanine is extremely loyal and has a brave and noble temperament. Its speed exceeds 60 mph, and it's capable of running 1,000 miles in a day and night.
