Vileplume

Vileplume is the evolved form of Gloom, a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Flower Pokémon. Vileplume's toxic pollen triggers atrocious allergy attacks. That's why it is advisable never to approach any attractive flowers in a jungle, however pretty they may be.
