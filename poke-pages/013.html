Weedle

Weedle is a Bug/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Hairy Bug Pokémon. Weedle has a sharp, toxic stinger on its head and a voracious appetite for leaves.

Height: 0.3m | Weight: 3.2kg

Inherent Ability: Shield Dust

Weakness:
