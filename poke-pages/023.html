Ekans

Ekans is a Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Snake Pokémon. Ekans curls itself up in a spiral while it rests. Assuming this position allows it to quickly respond to a threat from any direction with a glare from its upraised head.

Height: 2.0m | Weight: 6.9kg

Inherent Ability: Intimidate | Shed Skin (Hidden Ability)

Type:

  • Poison

Weakness:

  • Ground
  • Psychic
