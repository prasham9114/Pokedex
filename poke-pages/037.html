Vulpix

Vulpix is a Fire-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Fox Pokémon. Inside Vulpix's body burns a flame that never goes out. During the daytime, when the temperatures rise, this Pokémon releases flames from its mouth to prevent its body from growing too hot.

Height: 0.6m | Weight: 9.9kg

Inherent Ability: Flash Fire | Drought (Hidden Ability)
