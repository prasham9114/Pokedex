Raticate

Raticate is the evolved form of Rattata, a Normal-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is known as the Mouse Pokémon. Raticate's sturdy fangs grow steadily. To keep them ground down, it gnaws on rocks and logs. It may even chew on the walls of houses.

Height: 0.7m | Weight: 18.5kg

Inherent Ability: Run Away | Guts (Hidden Ability)
